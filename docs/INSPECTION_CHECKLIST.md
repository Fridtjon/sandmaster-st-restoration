# Inspection Checklist

Use this checklist to assess the current condition of your Kyosho Sandmaster ST.

## How to Use This Checklist

Go through each section and mark items as:
- **OK** - Good condition, no action needed
- **WORN** - Functional but should be replaced soon
- **BROKEN** - Needs replacement before running
- **MISSING** - Part is not present
- **N/A** - Not applicable or can't assess

## Next Steps After Inspection

1. **If engine is seized:** Research engine rebuild or replacement
2. **If engine turns freely:** Order glow plugs and fuel
3. **Order replacement fuel lines** (universal silicone fits)
4. **Test radio system** with fresh batteries
5. **Take detailed photos** of any damage/wear for forum help
6. **Post on RC10Talk** with findings for community advice
